OUR RESTAURANT BULGOGI

Bulgogi is one of the most well-known dishes in Korean cuisine, second only to Kimchi. The brand name "BULLGOGI" was inspired by our logo depicting a “Bull” combined with our sister restaurant "GOGI" and our aim is to create a warm, comfortable venue to drink and enjoy great tasting Korean food. BULLGOGIi's menu is characterized by a variety of authentic Korean dishes with a Western twist. Each of our tapas-sized dishes are created using natural Korean ingredients and traditional cooking techniques, perfect for sharing. We offer our guests a chance to try ‘table BBQ’. This means BULLGOGI is not just a place for eating, but also a place to experience Korean culture.
